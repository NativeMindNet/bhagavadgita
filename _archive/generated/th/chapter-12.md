## โศลก 12.1

### สันสกฤต

```
अर्जुन उवाच ।
एवं सततयुक्ता ये भक्तास्त्वां पर्युपासते ।
ये चाप्यक्षरमव्यक्तं तेषां के योगवित्तमाः ॥१॥
```

### การถอดเสียง

### คำแปล

> [EN] Arjuna inquired: Of those devotees who are constantly engaged in worshipping You (as Śyāmasundar, Your original humanlike form) with exclusive devotion, and those who worship the impersonal Brahma, who is the best knower of yoga?
