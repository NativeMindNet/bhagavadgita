Selfless duty performed as an offering to the Supreme Lord is called yajña or sacrifice O Arjuna all action performed for any other purpose is the cause of bondage in this world of repeated birth and death Therefore remaining unattached to the fruits of action perform all your duties in the spirit of such sacrifice Such selfless action is the way to begin devotional practice gradually realise the presence of the Lord and ultimately attain pure ujitoaji transcendental to the modes of material nature [nirguṇa bhakti]
