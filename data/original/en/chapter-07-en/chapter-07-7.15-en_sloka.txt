These four kinds of wrongdoers do not surrender unto Me: fruitive workers, comparable to animals; fallen persons who take the highest path, devotion, but later reject it, considering it inadequate or worthless; those whose knowledge covered by illusion, Māyā (despite their scriptural study, they consider that only the form of Lord Nārāyaṇ is worshippable, and they think that Lord Kṛṣṇa, Lord Rāma and other factual forms are merely mortal); and the demoniac (impersonalists who dismember My form by their arrows of false logic, resembling demons like Jarāsandha).
