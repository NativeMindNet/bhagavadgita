Using this weapon to cut down that illusory tree of one’s mundane existence which is stubbornly rooted in aversion to the Supreme Lord, one attains to the plane of no return, which is the lotus feet of the Lord. With pure devotion, one should approach the holy lotus feet of the Supreme Lord Viṣṇu for shelter, and pray, “I surrender unto the Original Person, the Supreme Lord of all, by whose illusory potency (māyā) this perpetual tree of the material world has appeared.”
